Performance Decline Over Time - LLM Deep Dive

Win Rate Dropped 24.6 Points; Skill Scores Declined 15% (Aug 6 - Sep 28)

Win Rate & Skill Score Trends (Weekly Analysis)

Win Rate: 64.29% → 39.64%

Skill Score: 4.10 → 3.50

1. Shift to Transactional Approach

Early period (Aug 6-15) prioritized authentic connections and systematic discovery. Late period (Sep 15-28) shifted to transactional interactions, reducing trust.

2. Less Name Usage & Personalization

Consistent use of customer names and personalized questions dropped significantly in later weeks, leading to lower customer engagement.

3. Failure to Secure Explicit Agreement

Reps failed to confirm resolution of objections or gain progressive agreement on price/timing, causing ambiguity at the close.

4. Missing Clear Closing Questions

Unlike the early period, later calls lacked clear closing questions and follow-up confirmations, resulting in missed commitments.

Immediate Interventions

  • Training: Focus on authentic connections & securing explicit agreement.

  • Protocol: Mandate name usage and personalized discovery questions.

  • Structure: Reinforce "permission to question" & emotional driver discovery.

  • Process: Implement clear closing checklists with confirmation steps.
