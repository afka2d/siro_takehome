Win Rate & Skill Score Trends (Weekly Analysis)

Win Rate: 64.29% → 39.64%
Skill Score: 4.10 → 3.50

1. Shift to Transactional Approach

Early period prioritized authentic connections. Late period shifted to transactional interactions, reducing trust.

2. Less Name Usage & Personalization

Consistent use of customer names and personalized questions dropped significantly, leading to lower engagement.

3. Failure to Secure Explicit Agreement

Reps failed to confirm resolution of objections or gain progressive agreement on price/timing, causing ambiguity.

4. Missing Clear Closing Questions

Later calls lacked clear closing questions and follow-up confirmations, resulting in missed commitments.

Immediate Interventions

  • Training on authentic connections & securing explicit agreement
  • Mandate name usage and personalized discovery questions
  • Reinforce "permission to question" & emotional driver discovery
  • Implement clear closing checklists with confirmation steps
